[[gnos]] Ignorantia  [[/gnos]]   marks the label's first Various Artists compilation. The release brings together six producers across six tracks, presenting a spectrum of darker, driving, and cinematic techno, with a strong focus on texture and atmosphere. Each track is immersive, balancing physical impact with structural depth.

[[gnos]]  Ignorantia  [[/gnos]]  represents the starting point, the awareness of not yet knowing. This initial chapter establishes the foundation; it reflects a state in which collective consciousness exists before clear structure, before fixed answers. What follows will expand, question, and ultimately transform this framework.
